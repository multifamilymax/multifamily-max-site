CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT
Relating to the Offering of 309–313 W Milwaukee St, Janesville, Wisconsin

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date of the undersigned's form submission, between:

Multifamily Max, a commercial real estate brokerage operating in the State of Wisconsin, represented by Max Fiascone
(hereinafter referred to as the "Disclosing Party")

and the individual or entity submitting the OM request form
(hereinafter referred to as the "Receiving Party").

Purpose

The Disclosing Party is making available certain non-public financial, operational, and marketing information relating to the real property located at 309–313 W Milwaukee St, Janesville, Wisconsin 53548 (the "Property"), including but not limited to an Offering Memorandum, rent roll, financial summaries, and supporting documentation (collectively, "Confidential Information"). This Agreement governs the Receiving Party's use of that information for the sole purpose of evaluating a potential acquisition of the Property.

1. Confidential Information

  1. 1.1 Definition. Confidential Information means any non-public financial, operational, tenant, marketing, or structural information relating to the Property provided by the Disclosing Party in connection with this Agreement, whether provided in written, electronic, or verbal form.
  2. 1.2 Obligations. The Receiving Party agrees to treat all Confidential Information with reasonable care and to use it solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential acquisition of the Property. The Receiving Party shall not use the Confidential Information for any other commercial purpose.

2. Non-Disclosure

  1. 2.1 Restriction. The Receiving Party agrees not to share, distribute, reproduce, or disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, except to the Receiving Party's legal, financial, or professional advisors who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations and have a need to know the information for the purpose of evaluating the Property.
  2. 2.2 Exceptions. These restrictions do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party; or (c) is required to be disclosed by applicable law, regulation, or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party prompt prior written notice of such required disclosure.

3. No Representation or Warranty

The Disclosing Party makes no representations or warranties, express or implied, as to the accuracy or completeness of the Confidential Information. The Receiving Party agrees to conduct its own independent due diligence and to verify any information material to its investment decision. The Disclosing Party shall have no liability to the Receiving Party arising from or relating to the use of the Confidential Information.

4. Return or Destruction of Information

Upon the request of the Disclosing Party, or upon the Receiving Party's decision not to proceed with the acquisition of the Property, the Receiving Party agrees to promptly destroy or permanently delete all Confidential Information and any copies thereof, in whatever form or medium, and to certify such destruction upon request.

5. Term

This Agreement is effective from the date of the Receiving Party's form submission and remains in force for a period of two (2) years, unless earlier terminated by written mutual agreement of both Parties.

6. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Wisconsin, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Rock County, Wisconsin.

7. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the confidentiality of the subject matter described herein and supersedes all prior oral or written discussions and understandings relating thereto. This Agreement may not be amended except by written agreement signed by both Parties.

Acceptance

By submitting the OM request form and checking the confidentiality agreement box, the Receiving Party acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to be legally bound by the terms of this Agreement as of the date of submission.
