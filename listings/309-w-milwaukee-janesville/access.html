Deal Story

309–313 W Milwaukee St — Janesville, WI

We are pleased to announce the successful sale of 309–313 W Milwaukee St, a 7-unit mixed-use property in the heart of downtown Janesville, Wisconsin.

The seller came to us through a prior relationship with a hard deadline: just over two months to close in order to meet a 1031 exchange timeline. We moved immediately, launching the property across all major platforms within 10 days.

Built in 1900, the property features three ground-floor commercial storefronts and four residential apartments above. A building with this much history in a downtown corridor requires the right buyer — someone who understands the long-term value of a well-positioned mixed-use asset and has the vision to carry it forward. After multiple showings, we accepted an offer three weeks after launch and closed on time, protecting the seller's exchange.

This transaction reflects the continued demand for mixed-use assets in Wisconsin's secondary markets, where quality downtown inventory is limited and long-term upside is real.
